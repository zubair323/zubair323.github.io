About Me

I have joined the Robotics Institute (CMU) as a postdoctoral researcher. I will be working on Triamisu, an interdisciplinary project, with four advisers/collaborators: Dr. Anthony Tomasic (Language Technologies), Dr. Charles Garrod (Sofwtare Engineering), Dr. Aaron Steinfeld (Robotics), and Dr. John Zimmermann (HCI).
Earlier I was a research fellow with the Center for Identity at the University of Texas at Austin where I worked with Prof. Suzanne Barber from August 2013 to September 2014.
I completed my PhD with Dr. Sarfarz Khurshid at the University of Texas at Austin in August 2014. Research work towards my PhD was completed in July 2013.

Research Interests

My research focuses on enhancing reliability of computer systems through software verification, validation and testing. My work directly enables advanced manufacturing, enhances software security, and improves fault tolerance of robots and autonomous computing agents. I am interested in leveraging the advances in verification, constraint-solving, cloud-computing, big-data and machine-learning for improving techniques in program transformation, program understanding, security and automated debugging.
